Section 06
Action Plan
Six priority actions mapped to the data above.

1. Fix Conversion Tracking

Zero leads captured across 74 engaged accounts. Deploy Lead Gen Forms at BOF targeting the warm pool, implement thank-you page conversion events, or establish manual pipeline matching. This is the single highest-impact action.

2. Connect CRM Data

74 accounts are clicking, but we cannot determine which are in pipeline. Request read-only CRM access or a manual export. Cross-referencing engaged accounts against pipeline transforms "clicks" into "pipeline accounts touched by LinkedIn."

3. Deploy Company Exclusion Lists

Add exclusion lists for non-ICP industries (sports, banking, education management) and specific high-impression zero-click accounts like SoFi Stadium (692 imp, 0 clicks) and Citizens Business Bank (458 imp, 0 clicks). Zero cost, immediate impact on targeting quality.

4. Concentrate TOF Budget

The 200K Manufacturing audience at $5K/mo produces only 4.7% penetration. Reallocate toward proven segments: Food and Bev Manufacturing (0.45% CTR), Chemical Manufacturing (0.50% CTR), Transportation Equipment (0.50% CTR).

5. Activate Sales on Warm Accounts

Share the top 20 ICP-fit engaged accounts (Huntsman Corp, Cardinal Glass, OCI Global, Altium Packaging, etc.) with Simple Green's sales team for direct outreach. These accounts have demonstrated intent through repeated ad engagement.

6. DemandSense Recommendations

Use Audience Tuning to suppress non-ICP segments. Revisit WebID after conversion tracking is fixed to identify named contacts behind company signals. Use Revenue Attribution once CRM is connected to tie ad exposure to deals.
