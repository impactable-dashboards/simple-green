Third-Party Fit and Intent Signals
Enrichment Signals for Account Scoring
Signals beyond LinkedIn native targeting. Use to build scored target account lists for ABM upload or outbound activation.

Regulatory Exposure

Facilities using methylene chloride products, subject to EPA TSCA ban. High urgency for replacement. Anti-Spatter is the direct product play.

Source: EPA compliance databases, SDS registries, industry trade publications

OSHA Recordables

Companies with recent OSHA recordables from chemical exposure. Simple Green's 0/0/0/0 NFPA/HMIS rating directly addresses the pain. $40K+ per incident creates budget justification.

Source: OSHA inspection database (public), insurance industry reports

LEED/Green Certification

Facilities pursuing LEED certification or green procurement mandates. Green Seal GS-37 certification qualifies Simple Green for LEED credits.

Source: USGBC project directory, company sustainability reports

Multi-Site Operations

Companies with 5+ facilities. Higher consolidation value. One vendor replacing 4-5 regional chemical suppliers. Operations and Procurement personas feel this pain most.

Source: D&B, company websites, Crunchbase

Hiring: EHS/Safety Roles

Companies hiring EHS Managers, Safety Directors, or Compliance Officers. Indicates investment in safety infrastructure. New hires audit existing chemical inventory.

Source: LinkedIn job postings, Indeed, Glassdoor

Trade Events

Attendees of ISSA Show, National Safety Council Congress, WEFTEC, Pack Expo, IFT Food Expo. Concentrates industrial cleaning buyers in one list.

Source: Event registration lists, badge scan data (if client attends)

Diversey/Ecolab Displacement

Companies currently using Diversey or Ecolab who are mid-market (201-5K employees). These companies overpay for enterprise-grade service. Simple Green is the right-sized alternative.

Source: Technographic data (Clay, ZoomInfo), vendor review sites
