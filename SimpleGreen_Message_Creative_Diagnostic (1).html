Section 04
Untested White Space Angles
From the Competitive White Space Analysis: 5 positioning gaps no competitor owns. None have been tested in ad creative.

Regulatory Compliance

Opportunity: HIGH

EPA methylene chloride ban, OSHA exposure limits, LEED credit requirements. No competitor positions against named regulations.

"That degreaser in your facility might be one OSHA inspection away from a citation."
Best Persona Fit: EHS Managers, Facilities Managers, Plant Managers

Worker Safety / OSHA Incident Prevention

Opportunity: HIGH

Connect Simple Green's 0/0/0/0 NFPA/HMIS rating to reducing OSHA recordables. $40K+ per incident vs. product price premium.

"One chemical injury costs $40,000+. Your degreaser's safety rating costs $0 to check."
Best Persona Fit: Safety Managers, EHS Managers, Plant Managers, Operations Leaders

Chemical Consolidation / SKU Reduction

Opportunity: HIGH

One concentrate replaces 5-10 specialized cleaners. Reduces SDS count, training, storage, vendor complexity.

"How many SDSs does your facility manage? Most have 3x more than they need."
Best Persona Fit: Purchasing Managers, Procurement, Operations Leaders

Heritage + Innovation (50 Years)

Opportunity: MEDIUM

50-year track record in industrial. No eco-competitor matches this tenure. Zep has age but not safety. ECOS has eco but not industrial.

"50 years in industrial. Zero safety incidents from our formula."
Best Persona Fit: All personas (trust signal)

Facilities Intelligence / Tools

Opportunity: MEDIUM

SSA tool, Dilution Calculator, Cost Per Gallon Calculator already exist but are not marketed. Mid-market gap is wide open.

"Run a free Safer Solutions Assessment and see what your cleaning program actually costs."
Best Persona Fit: Procurement, Operations, Facilities Managers
Zero white space angles have been tested in paid creative. The competitive analysis identified 5 positioning gaps. The top 3 (Regulatory Compliance, Worker Safety, SKU Consolidation) are HIGH opportunity and map directly to the personas already engaging: QA Leaders, Operations Leaders, Safety/EHS, and Procurement. These should be the first creative tests in the next 90 days.

Section 07
Creative Action Plan
Prioritized actions based on diagnostic findings.
Priority 1: Deploy Lead Gen Forms at BOF. Zero leads captured. This is the conversion mechanism. Offer options: Safer Solutions Assessment for Operations, Anti-Spatter sample for EHS, LEED compliance guide for Facilities. Test immediately on the warm pool.
Priority 2: Refresh all TOF image creative immediately. 6 months past benchmark lifespan. Replace with white-space messaging: (1) Regulatory compliance angle for EHS/Facilities, (2) Worker safety / OSHA cost angle for Safety/Plant Managers, (3) SKU consolidation angle for Procurement/Operations. This simultaneously fixes fatigue AND tests untested positioning.
Priority 3: Launch Thought Leader ads. 3x CTR benchmark. VP and CXO engagement is currently minimal (3.5% of clicks). TL format puts content from a real person in-feed, which earns attention from executive audiences. Source: Simple Green founder story, 50-year heritage, or industry safety content.
Priority 4: Add non-branded industrial terms to Google. Google currently runs 100% branded capture. Adding terms like "LEED compliant cleaner," "OSHA safe industrial degreaser," "EPA Safer Choice industrial," and "replace methylene chloride anti-spatter" would generate net-new demand that LinkedIn cannot capture alone.
Priority 5: Plan MOF video refresh. CTR is still strong (0.69%) so this is not urgent, but at 11 months it is approaching the outer edge. Develop new video assets: Clean Building walkthrough, Anti-Spatter demo, or facilities consolidation story. Have assets ready before CTR decline begins.
Certification Precision Note (from client). Simple Green's cleaner IS Safer Choice Certified. Disinfectants are NOT. Previous ad copy implied disinfectants were certified, and the client corrected it. All future creative must be precise: "EPA Safer Choice certified cleaners and degreasers" not "EPA Safer Choice certified products" generically.
